Ingredients:
 - Rib eye steak
 - 1 knob of butter (not spreadable)
 - a dash of vegetable oil
 - Sea salt
 - Pepper

Directions:
  1. Take the steak out of the fridge and pat it down with kitchen towel, removing any excess liquid.
  2. Put your griddle pan on a very high heat, add the oil to the pan
  3. Generously season both sides of the steak with lots of salt and pepper
  4. Once the pan is on the point of smoking, add the steak to the pan with the butter
  5. Cook for 2 minutes on each side on the highest heat, try not to fiddle with it
  6. Baste the steak with the butter and oil in the pan using a spoon
  7. Once it's cooked to you liking, remove it from the pan and let it rest for 3 or 4 minutes before serving.
